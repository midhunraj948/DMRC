About Us

This is not a two wheeler based group. Anyone interested in traveling can join this group. Then came the name Dharmadam Riders Club to make our country known. Our country has its own legacy. It was not intended to be an All Kerala Club. Maybe if we were traveling to Kerala or India, when we needed help, Having people in the know can help you on the journey. This is why riders from other districts or travel lovers can join the club. The purpose of our club is to protect the environment and to comply with traffic laws. Kannur is not just a country known for its political killing. There are a lot of good young travel lovers and young people in Dharmadam, Kannur too. That is my goal with this club. This is what should be noted. Our club is the answer and example of many people's doubts that his bike is not just for work but also for long haul. This is their fellowship; For those who love to travel and love nature, that's it
